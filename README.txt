GAME RULE:

There will be 3 special rocks in this game, 2 rocks are for unlocking invisible platforms and 1 for the penguin girl at the end to win the game. Collect all 3 rocks to process the game.
Gaining points is not from collecting tokens as tokens have their own special functions. Gaining points is from killing the enemies and the boss by collecting bullet bags. 
There is only 2 bullet bags in this game, each one activates for 10 seconds. 

Note:

Only the second bullet bag and the boss have fixed position, it is to make sure the boss is at the end of the game and the bullet bag is near the boss so player can use it to kill the boss easily and immediately. The rest of the tokens and enemies have random postion, even the special rocks and the first bullet bag.
